Our mission is to foster a supportive and empathetic community where students can openly discuss mental health concerns, access resources, and find the support they need to thrive academically and personally.

Our Objectives




Creating a Safe and Supportive Space

We offer a confidential and welcoming environment for medical students to share their mental health experiences and challenges without fear of judgment.








Empowering Through Peer Counseling

We believe in the power of peer support. Our club trains medical students to become peer counselors, equipping them with the skills to offer empathetic and effective support to their peers.









Raising Awareness

We strive to break the stigma surrounding mental health issues by raising awareness and educating students about the importance of mental well-being through workshops, seminars, and campaigns.









Connecting with Professionals

We bridge the gap between students and mental health professionals, both on and off campus, ensuring that students have access to the best possible care and support.









Promoting Healthy Coping Mechanisms

Through various activities and programs, we encourage the adoption of healthy coping strategies and self-care practices, empowering students to manage stress and maintain their mental health.









Advocating for Supportive Policies

We actively advocate for policies and practices within the university that prioritize and enhance student mental health, aiming to create a more supportive academic environment.
